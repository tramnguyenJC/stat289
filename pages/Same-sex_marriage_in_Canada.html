Same-sex marriage was legalized across Canada by the Civil Marriage Act enacted on July 20 , 2005 . Court decisions, starting in 2003 , had already legalized the issue of marriage licences to same-sex couples in eight out of ten provinces and one of three territories, whose residents comprised about 90% of Canada's population; most legal benefits commonly associated with marriage had been extended to cohabiting same-sex couples since 1999 .
