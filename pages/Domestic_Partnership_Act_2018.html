Domestic Partnership Act 2018

The Domestic Partnership Act 2018 is an act of the Parliament of Bermuda . It provided for the creation of domestic partnerships for both same-sex and opposite-sex couples and prohibited same-sex marriage , making the Parliament of Bermuda the first legislature to abolish same-sex marriage after being initially legalised.
